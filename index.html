About Me

Hello! My name is Ripple and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet. My interest in web development started back in 2020 when I decided to try editing custom Tumblr themes — turns out hacking together a custom reblog button taught me a lot about HTML & CSS!

Fast-forward to today, I am a second-year student majoring in Computer Information Systems.

Throughout my journey as a developer, I've had the opportunity to work on various projects that have helped me develop a strong foundation in web technologies. I'm particularly passionate about creating intuitive user interfaces and solving complex problems through clean, efficient code.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or expanding my knowledge through online courses and tutorials.
